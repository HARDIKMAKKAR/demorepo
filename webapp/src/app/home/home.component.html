Overview

VisuoPrime home edition is a vision therapy software to treat eye problems such as amblyopia (lazy eye), strabismus (squint), vergence problems like convergence Insufficiency, depth perception issues, focusing problems and suppression at home.

Your eye doctor will diagnose your problems and prescribe the therapy accordingly. You can practice the therapy at home. Your progress at home will be monitored and guided by your doctor. Doctors can change the parameter levels and the therapies as and when required.
